title: B.3.a. Full-Time and Part-Time Enrollment Status
date: 05/18/2026
---
During Middlebury Institute Online terms (e.g., Spring, Summer, Fall), a student is considered three-quarter time when enrolled in six or more credits per term, and less than half-time when enrolled in three credits per term. The regular course load for students in Middlebury Institute Online programs is six credits per term.
