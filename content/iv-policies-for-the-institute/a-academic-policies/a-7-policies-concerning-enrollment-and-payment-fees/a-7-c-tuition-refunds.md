Weekend workshops and other brief courses dropped by 4 P.M. on the last business day a week before they begin will receive full refund. After that deadline, there is no refund.

If the student has been billed full tuition but has not paid in full, her/his tuition account will be credited for the difference between full-time tuition and the per-credit cost.
